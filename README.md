# hospital-management-system  using frontend + backend 
Patient Registration  :
This module is used to register new patients in the hospital system. It stores basic patient details such as name, age, gender, contact number, address, and medical history. The data is saved in MongoDB and can be updated or viewed anytime by authorized staff.

Appointment Scheduling  :
This module allows patients or staff to book appointments with doctors. It manages doctor availability, appointment date and time, and prevents scheduling conflicts. Appointment details are stored securely and can be modified or canceled when required.

Electronic Health Records (EHR) :
The EHR module maintains digital medical records of patients, including diagnosis, prescriptions, lab reports, and treatment history. Doctors can easily access and update patient health data, improving accuracy and continuity of care.

Payment Management :
This module handles patient billing and payments. It records consultation fees, treatment costs, test charges, and payment status. It supports multiple payment methods and helps generate invoices and financial reports.

Inventory Management :
This module manages hospital inventory such as medicines, medical equipment, and supplies. It tracks stock levels, updates inventory usage, and alerts staff when items are low to ensure uninterrupted hospital operations.
